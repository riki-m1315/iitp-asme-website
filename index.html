Overview

The ASME Student Chapter at IIT Patna is a vibrant community of budding engineers driven by curiosity and collaboration. Our chapter provides a platform where students can explore innovation in mechanical engineering, exchange ideas, and connect with industry and academia.

Motto: Organize events and hands-on activities that train the youth in mechanical engineering and allied skills — equipping them with technical expertise, creativity, and leadership to solve real-world problems.

We host workshops, technical talks, competitions, and outreach programs designed to build skills and confidence among students. Members get mentorship, access to resources, and opportunities to showcase projects at conferences and exhibitions.

Join us to learn design, manufacturing, thermofluids, and leadership — and to build things that matter.
